Air Canada poster
This Project was focused on creating an Air Canada Poster to a destination of your choice. The main challenge for me with this project was finding the location I wanted to focus the poster on. Another challenge was finding a unique perspective to make the poster more interesting.
70-Below
Niagara College hosted a design-a-thon. We worked in teams to create a unique identity and packaging. The owner of 70 Below was looking to re-brand their corporate identity, and re-design their packaging and website. Each group had to work for 8 hours to reach the client’s expectations. My group came up with this design concept. The main goal was to target those who enjoy hiking and the outdoors.
Ali Book cover

Crazy D's
